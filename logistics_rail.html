RAIL

About us / Logistics / Rail

Rail freight is highly secured, fast, prompt and most ecologically friendly mode of transportation cargoes of any type and size. We offer solutions for full and partly loaded containerized commodities, wagons and platforms at any destination within Europe, Asia and worldwide.

Our secure and professional and experienced services include:

- Reliable door - to - door solutions for single container, FTL, LCL, block train
- Cost effective routing meeting short transit time and flexibility requirements
- Cross docking- customs clearance and complete documentation arrangements at departure and destination locations
- Consolidation
- Proper and secure equipment for loading/ unloading
- Transport insurance
- 24/7 track and trace
- Safety and monitoring equipment

“SILKY WAY” regular services for LCL and FTL cargoes from Asia and Europe and back:

- Guaranteed weekly tailor made service for LCL departures from several destinations in China covering neighbor countries, Vietnam, South Korea and Uzbekistan
- Fast lead time for direct train, multimode rail-road mode
- Cargo collection from several vendors or agents
- Consolidation, storage, packing, labeling and cargo inspection
- Complete documentation arrangements for import and export, customs clearance, licenses and certificates meeting all requirements and local regulations
- Multimodal rail–road services and trucking

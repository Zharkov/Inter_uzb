About us / Logistics / 4 PL

HLD  offers tailor-made supply chain management solutions and 4PL services, which enable our clients to concentrate on their business growth and innovation while we are completely covering supply chain and logistics issues.

We are optimizing the service in concern with the client’s needs and goals, considering the budgets, terms, other key factors and client’s requirements

- individual solutions and offerings
- extensive 4PL solutions
- insurance, certification, customs clearance, packing and labeling, documents management worldwide
- subcontractors quality control
- risks optimization distribution
- «door — to — door» service worldwide

Being the global forwarder, we provide industry-specific ready-made solutions whatever our client's business is:

- pharmaceuticals, medical equipment and healthcare
- oil and gas
- food and beverage fashion
- landscape and plants
- constructing and production
- trade and manufacturing

HLD

Founded over 20 years ago, HLD — is an international service company headquartered in Hungary with leading expertise in international trade, construction and logistics. Our offices are located in 4 countries:

Our company combines unmatched experience and specialized skills across more than 50 industries. We are assisting clients to become the next and best versions of themselves, HLD is dedicated to connecting countries and cultures delivering the best solutions for international commerce, industrial and private constructing and logistics. By embracing the power of change HLD is able to create value and shared success for its clients, people, shareholders and partners

HLD primarily comprises of 3 operational Divisions:

Trade

HLD — is a full service import/ export and procurement company that operates throughout the world and powered by professional approach to sales and distribution, we provide a structured, effective system for moving products from the seller to the customer.

Construction

HLD operates more than 20 years in the European construction industry. We are experts in construction of all building and infrastructure types, with experts in all aspects of engineering and mechanical infrastructure networks, systems and more.

Logistics

HLD  – is a 4Pl logistics operator with over 20 years experience in international logistics and transportation. Our clients feel confident when shipping the cargo with highly trained and experienced team. We connect the dots between carriers, ports, customs, trucking and rail providers, vendors and agents. We ensure the best solutions and answer highest shipping requirements.
